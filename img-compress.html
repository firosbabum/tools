Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can compress these formats or convert between them.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size is 10MB to ensure fast processing in your browser.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

What's the best format for web use?

WebP generally offers the best compression with good quality. JPEG is good for photos, while PNG is better for graphics with transparency.
